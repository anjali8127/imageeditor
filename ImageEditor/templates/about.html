About ImageEditor

Hello, welcome to ImageEditor – The next generation photo editor.

Trouble editing picture, adding effects, adding filters & compressing them? That’s what we are here to help you with. We provide various features to assist your photo editing needs. Few of them being
Editing – With ImageEditor, you can now easily edit brightness, contrast, sharpness, blur, denoise resize & rotate according to your choice.
Effects – ImageEditor lets you choose from a number of predetermined effects which helps you change the coloring and look of your pictures.
Compression – Trouble uploading pictures on applications or other websites due to high image sizes? Don’t worry, ImageEditor brings in a new feature of compressing your images according to your needs. You can decide the percentages by which you want to compress your images may it be by 25%, 50% or 75%. We are working on getting more and more features for you. Hang on with us to have an access to new upcoming features & feel free to let us know if you want anything further from us.
